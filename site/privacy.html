Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you use our website or mobile application, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Home address
  • Phone number

This information is collected when you register for an account or update your account profile.

How We Use Your Information

The personal information we collect is used for the purposes of:

  • Creating and managing your user account
  • Providing the services and features you request
  • Communicating with you about your account or our services

We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the confidentiality and integrity of your personal information. However, no security system is impenetrable and we cannot guarantee the security of our systems 100%.

Your Rights & Choices

You can access and update your personal information at any time by logging into your account. You may also contact us to request access to, correct, or delete any personal information that you have provided to us.

Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your WCR Studios account and associated personal data, please submit a request using the form linked below:

Request Account Deletion

Once you submit the account deletion request, all of your personal information including your name, email, address, phone number and any other data associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. Please note that deletion requests are irreversible.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
