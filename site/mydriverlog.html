MyDriverLog App

A user-friendly app designed to help parents track their teen's driving hours, ensuring they meet the requirements for obtaining a driver's license.

App Features

Intuitive Dashboard

The MyDriverLog app provides an intuitive dashboard that gives parents and teens an overview of their driving progress. The dashboard displays key statistics such as total hours driven, night hours, and days remaining until the target is reached.

Easy Logging

Log Drives with Ease

With MyDriverLog, logging driving hours is a breeze. The app allows users to quickly enter the date, start and end times, and weather conditions for each driving session. Users can also add notes for each entry, such as the route taken or skills practiced.

Parental Oversight

Monitor Multiple Drivers

MyDriverLog supports families with more than one driving teen by allowing parents to monitor each teen's driving progress individually. The app provides separate logs for each driver, ensuring that all teens are meeting the necessary experience and state requirements for obtaining a driver's license.

Real-Time Tracking

Track Drives as They Happen

The MyDriverLog app features a built-in drive timer that allows users to track their driving sessions in real-time. Simply start the timer when the driving session begins and stop it when finished. The app will automatically log the date, start and end times, and duration of the session.
